How to Apply申請流程

Your path to becoming
a SEAS Research Scholar成為SEAS學術研究學者的路徑

STAGE
1
📝
Online Application線上申請
Submit Your Application提交申請表單
The first step to becoming a scholar is to apply! Each cohort has an application deadline, so ensure that you submit your application before that date.成為學者的第一步就是申請！每梯次均設有申請截止日期，請確保在截止日期前提交申請。
STAGE
2
🗣️
Interview Round面試環節
Share Your Research Ideas分享您的研究想法
If your application is shortlisted, you move to the interview round, where you will have an interview with a SEAS team member and elaborate on your research interests and ideas.若您的申請入圍，將進入面試環節，與SEAS團隊成員進行訪談，詳細說明您的研究興趣與想法。
STAGE
3
🤝
Mentor Matching導師配對
Find Your Perfect Mentor找到最適合您的導師
After your interview, the SEAS team will work on finding you the best mentor match based on your interests and aims. You will also be notified of your application decision at this stage.面試結束後，SEAS團隊將根據您的興趣和目標，為您找到最適合的導師。您也將在此階段收到申請結果通知。
STAGE
4
🚀
Start Your Research!開始您的研究！
Kickstart Your Journey啟動您的學術旅程
Once you are aligned with your proposed mentor and have fulfilled all the logistical requirements, you can officially kickstart the journey of a SEAS Research Scholar!在與您的導師達成共識並完成所有前置作業後，您即可正式開啟成為SEAS學術研究學者的旅程！

Ready to build your academic identity? 準備好建立您的學術身份了嗎？
